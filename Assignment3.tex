\section*{\centerline{Swamp reclamation in Uganda}}

\subsection*{Problem statement}

There is has been an increase in swamp reclamtion in uganda
this is mainly due to the inefficiency of NEMA which has also most probably taken part in the land grabbing
this has also led to the worsening of weather conditions in the country ie longer dry seasons which usually destroy property

\subsection*{Objectives}
\begin{itemize}
\item to Sensitize the public of the dangers of swamp reclamation
\item to be able to identify a solution to the problem
\item to learn about the causes of its increase

\end{itemize}
